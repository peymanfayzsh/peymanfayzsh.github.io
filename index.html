About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the Research Centre in Intelligent Technologies (CiTIUS), University of Santiago de Compostela, funded by the EU’s Horizon 2020 Marie Skłodowska-Curie project MENELAOSNT. My research focuses on CMOS time-of-flight image sensors, computational sensing, and micro/nanofabrication.

What I'm Doing

  • Analog/Mixed-Signal IC Design

    Designing and simulating integrated circuits for advanced imaging systems.

  • CMOS Image Sensors

    Developing CMOS image sensors with 2D/3D and ToF capabilities.

  • Microelectronics

    Exploring microelectronics for sensing, imaging, and wearable technologies.

  • Micro/Nanofabrication

    Fabrication of stretchable electronics, MEMS, and biosensors.
